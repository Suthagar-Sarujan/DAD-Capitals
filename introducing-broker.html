What is IB?

An Introducing Broker (IB) acts as a trusted intermediary connecting new clients to our brokerage firm. The IB introduces clients and, based on their trading volume, earns commissions from the brokerage. This partnership model empowers IBs to build a sustainable income by leveraging their network and expertise in trading.

Who is the Introducing Broker (IB) Program designed for?

Whether you are a trading educator, influencer, financial consultant, or community leader, the IB program offers you a powerful way to monetize your connections. Ideal for those passionate about finance and trading, this program allows you to earn commissions while providing valuable services and education to your clients.
