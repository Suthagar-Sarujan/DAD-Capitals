Our Trading Conditions

Product Details

We offer a variety of financial products including Forex, CFDs on indices, commodities, cryptocurrencies, shares, and ETFs. Our products are designed to provide you with diverse opportunities for trading and investing.

  • Access to 40+ currency pairs in Forex.
  • Indices CFDs tracking major global stock indices.
  • Commodities including precious metals and energy products.
  • Cryptocurrency CFDs covering top digital assets.
  • Shares and ETFs CFDs from major global markets.

Trading Hours

Forex trading is available 24 hours a day from Monday to Friday, allowing you to trade at almost any time. Other products follow the official market hours of their respective exchanges, ensuring accurate pricing and liquidity.

  • Forex: Sunday 22:00 GMT to Friday 22:00 GMT.
  • Indices & Commodities: Market-specific hours.
  • Cryptocurrencies: Generally 24/7 but subject to liquidity.
  • Shares & ETFs: Follow stock exchange trading hours.

Holidays & Orders

  • On public holidays, trading hours may vary or be closed for certain products.
  • We execute orders efficiently with no requotes under normal market conditions.
  • Holiday schedules published in advance.
  • Market orders, limit orders, stop orders supported.
  • Order execution speed optimized for low latency.
  • No requotes during normal market conditions.

Risk Management

  • Negative Balance Protection: Automatic balance reset to zero in extreme market events.
  • Margin calls issued to maintain required equity levels.
  • Forex leverage up to 1:30 for retail clients.
  • CFDs leverage varies by asset class.

Forex Trading Conditions

Overnight Financing

Forex positions held overnight incur swap fees based on the interest rate differential between the currencies involved. Financing rates are updated daily and disclosed transparently.

  • Swap rates depend on currency pairs and market conditions.
  • Positive or negative swap can apply.
  • Swap rollover time at 24:00 GMT daily.
  • Clients can check current swap rates in the platform.

Execution & Spreads

  • Variable spreads starting from 0.1 pips on major pairs.
  • No commission on standard accounts.
  • Instant execution with no dealing desk intervention.

Indices, Commodities & Crypto CFDs

Overnight Financing

CFDs on indices, commodities, and cryptocurrencies are subject to overnight financing fees. These fees compensate for the cost of holding positions past trading hours.

  • Financing charges depend on the underlying asset.
  • Fees applied at rollover times specified per contract.
  • Details available on product specification pages.

Contracts' Rollover Dates

  • Rollover typically monthly or quarterly depending on the asset.
  • Clients notified ahead of rollover dates.
  • Positions automatically transferred to new contracts.

Leverage & Margins

  • Indices CFDs leverage up to 1:20
  • Commodities CFDs leverage up to 1:10
  • Crypto CFDs leverage up to 1:2

Shares & ETF CFDs

Overnight Financing

Overnight fees apply for CFDs on shares and ETFs held past trading hours. These reflect the borrowing costs of the underlying shares.

  • Financing rates updated regularly.
  • Fees depend on the specific share or ETF.
  • Transparent disclosure provided on platform.

Dividends

Dividends are credited to long positions and debited from short positions. This reflects the economic benefit or cost of holding shares through dividend dates.

  • Dividend adjustments calculated based on official announcements.
  • Details shown on account statements and platform.

Corporate Actions

  • Stock splits, mergers and other corporate actions handled according to exchange rules.
  • Clients notified of any adjustments to positions.
