Risk Disclosure

At DAD Capitals, we prioritize transparency. Trading forex, CFDs, and other financial instruments carries substantial risk and may not be suitable for all investors. We strongly advise Clients to review our full Risk Disclosure before trading.

Market Risk

Trading financial instruments such as Forex, CFDs, cryptocurrencies, indices, and commodities involves significant risk. Prices can move unpredictably due to news, economic events, geopolitical instability, or changes in market sentiment. You may lose part or all of your investment.

Leverage Risk

Leverage allows you to control larger positions with a small capital outlay. However, it magnifies both potential profits and potential losses. A small market movement can lead to large losses, including amounts exceeding your initial investment.

Liquidity Risk

Some instruments may have low trading volumes, leading to wider spreads and difficulty in executing trades at your desired price. Under volatile conditions, even major instruments can face temporary illiquidity.

Technical Risks

Online trading involves risks associated with technology such as internet disconnection, latency issues, software failure, or device malfunctions. DAD Capitals is not responsible for losses resulting from technical problems beyond our control.

Regulatory Risk

Changes in laws, regulations, or policies by governments or regulatory bodies may impact your trading activities, affect market conditions, or restrict access to certain financial instruments.

No Guarantee of Profit

Trading involves speculation and there are no guarantees of profit. Past performance of financial instruments or strategies does not guarantee future returns. All trading decisions are made at your own risk.

Suitability

Our services may not be suitable for all investors. You must assess your financial situation, risk appetite, and trading experience before opening an account. Seek independent financial advice if needed.
